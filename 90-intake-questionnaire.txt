Plana - Project Intake Questionnaire (Human-readable)

Purpose
- This file is for humans (planners/PMs) to quickly lock down the planning context.
- Fill this first, then update:
  - 00-competitive-and-policy-research.md
  - 01-product-brief.md
  - 02-requirements.md
  - 03-flow-and-ux.md (Mermaid)
  - 04-data-and-policy.md

How to use
1) Answer the questions below.
2) Convert your answers into the official docs (00~04 + shared logs).
3) When the topic changes later, re-run this questionnaire quickly and update 00 (research) first.

Section A. Topic / scope
- What are we building? (1 sentence)
- Who is it for? (user personas)
- What is NOT in scope? (explicit exclusions)
- Success criteria (what does “done” mean?)

Section B. Deliverables / outputs
- What must be exported/archived? (ZIP, docs, diagrams, etc.)
- Where will outputs be stored? (local, remote drive, WebDAV, etc.)

Section C. Data & policy (minimum)
- Data categories: what data do we store/process?
- Sensitivity: any PII? secrets? confidential docs?
- Retention: default retention + deletion policy + exceptions
- Training: are AI prompts/outputs used for training? (default should be “no training” for enterprise posture)
- Subprocessors: any external vendors/providers? list them.
- Permission boundary: what is the unit of access? (office/user/project/document)
- Admin controls needed: export on/off, retention policies, audit logs, etc.

Section D. Workflow / gates
- When do we consider the flow approved? Who approves?
- What decisions must be logged? (shared/30_decisions.md)
- What risks must be tracked? (shared/risk.md)

Section E. Competitive & policy research (for 00 doc)
- List at least 6 comparable products/services (prefer KR first).
- For each, capture policy patterns:
  - Training
  - Retention/Deletion
  - Subprocessors
  - Admin controls
  - Permissioning

Notes
- Keep this file as a human scratchpad; the SSoT is the Markdown docs in product-description/.
